09 / 44 Quality Metrics · Three Contexts

The resistance transcriptome is a selected regulatory state

Why C1+C2 are noisier

  • Initial-diagnosis FLT3-ITD A.M.L. samples carry every confounding co-mutation: DNMT3A, IDH1/2, TET2, NPM1, RUNX1, plus the FLT3-ITD itself
  • Heterogeneous transcriptional background
  • Drives high confounding rates (82.5% / 89.9%)

Why C3 is cleaner

  • Resistant cells have already passed the bottleneck of FLT3 inhibitor selection
  • Only gene programs that survived selection remain active
  • DAG is smaller (9,193 edges), pathway-richer (217 nodes), per-edge more interpretable
  • Database evidence 28.0% vs 1.1% in C1
Implication: Tier D acquired-resistance findings (POLR2C, DPAGT1, LINC00494, RRM2, SH3BP2, GINS1) carry higher per-target causal confidence than Tier A or Tier C at the same nominal rank.

23 / 44 LINC00494 · Largest Single Effect, Strictest Gate

CMKLR1 · the FLT3-ITD dependency switch

The phenomenon

Effect flips direction by FLT3 status:

  • FLT3- patients: +0.182 protective
  • FLT3+ patients: −0.074 beneficial
  • Differential effect: −0.256 (largest in study)

The mechanism

CMKLR1 is the chemerin GPCR — Gαi-coupled, reduces cAMP, activates ERK.

In FLT3-: CMKLR1 signalling is adaptive (cell uses chemerin/ERK signalling).

In FLT3-ITD: STAT5/MAPK is saturated → CMKLR1 becomes a dependency, not an escape route.

Wet-lab confirmation: CMKLR1 siRNA in MV4-11 (FLT3-ITD+) vs OCI-AML3 (FLT3-ITD−). Selective killing of FLT3-ITD+ confirms the switch. NON_ESSENTIAL → siRNA is wet-lab safe.

The synergy artefact pattern

What the model says

WDR4 + EPC2 synergy index 9.004 — far higher than CCT8 + IKZF1 at 3.237.

Why isn't this the top combination?

What's really happening

WDR4 effect: −0.250 (beneficial)

EPC2 effect: +0.243 (harmful · protective node)

Combined effect: −0.061 ≈ near zero

Synergy index = ratio, not magnitude → opposite-direction cancellation produces inflated synergy without real combination benefit.

User-facing rule: only trust synergy index when the underlying combined effect is large in absolute magnitude. CCT8 + IKZF1 has both high synergy AND large combined effect. WDR4 + EPC2 is a model-flagged mathematical interaction, not a lead combination.

Four more caveats
